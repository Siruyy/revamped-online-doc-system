# Training Agenda

Use this agenda for a short role-based walkthrough.

## Session 1 — Students

- Register and wait for approval.
- Submit a document request.
- Upload a payment receipt.
- Track request status and notifications.
- Download allowed files.

## Session 2 — Admins

- Review and approve/deny requests.
- Validate or reject requirements.
- Review payment receipts.
- Move requests through processing, ready for pickup, and release gates.

## Session 3 — Departments

- Open department clearance queue.
- Review uploaded supporting files.
- Sign or deny a clearance.
- Update PNG signature.

## Session 4 — SuperAdmin

- Approve pending users.
- Create staff users.
- Review logs.
- Export reports.
- Confirm access restrictions.

## Closing Checklist

- [ ] Every role can log in using a training account.
- [ ] Participants know where to report issues.
- [ ] Support severity levels are explained.
- [ ] Manual verification notes are recorded.
