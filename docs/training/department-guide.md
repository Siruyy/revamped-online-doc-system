# Department Guide

## Clearance Queue

1. Open **Clearances**.
2. Review items assigned to your department role only.
3. Open a clearance record before signing or denying.

## Signing

1. Confirm the student uploaded the required supporting file.
2. Add remarks if needed.
3. Click **Mark as cleared**.
4. After signing, the same department cannot act on that clearance again.

## Denying

1. Use denial only when the student must resolve an issue.
2. Enter clear remarks with the required next action.
3. Submit the denial.

## Signature Setup

- Upload a PNG signature in the profile/signature area.
- Maximum size is 1 MB.
- Replace signatures when staff responsibility changes.

## Access Rules

- Department users can only act on their own department status column.
- Do not download or share private student files outside approved workflows.
